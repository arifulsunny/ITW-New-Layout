What is this track about

Not whether immersive enters education. But how responsibly we integrate it.

Immersive technology will shape how we learn, research, and build knowledge systems. The question is not whether it enters education, but how responsibly, sustainably, and intelligently we integrate it.

XR in education is moving beyond experimentation. Now we must ask harder questions: Are we building infrastructure that scales beyond pilots? Are we raising digital consumers, or digital makers?

What happens to European research once funding cycles end?

Focus areas

Three pillars. One responsibility.

Each focus area tackles a critical dimension of how immersive technology reshapes education, research, and public knowledge systems.

01

Infrastructure, Adoption & Standardisation

Platforms, interoperability, and scaling immersive environments beyond pilots. Building the technical foundations that let innovation persist after the funding ends.

02

Human-Centric AI & Immersive Design

Autonomy, meaningful learning, and human agency in AI-supported systems. Ensuring technology serves the learner — not the other way around.

03

Public Values, Sovereignty & Institutional Responsibility

Sustainability, governance, and European digital leadership. Defining who owns the infrastructure, who sets the standards, and who is accountable.

Inspiring Tomorrow's World

If immersive tech is to enhance life, education must define its standards, ethics, and long-term vision. This track ensures that innovation is not only exciting, but accountable.
