What is it

Most conferences tell you XR is the future.
We help you ship it on Monday.

Here's the thing. Plenty of companies have run an XR pilot. A VR training here, a digital twin demo there. Then it stalls. The pilot was great, the business case wasn't clear, IT had questions, and leadership moved on.

The Corporate Innovation Track exists to fix that gap. Not with another inspirational keynote—but with templates, roadmaps, and peer connections that actually move projects forward.

You'll leave with a vendor assessment framework. A business case template that's worked at NS and KLM. And—this matters more than any template—a network of people who've hit the same walls and found the doors.
