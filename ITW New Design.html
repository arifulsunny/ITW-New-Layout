What is it

Not an exhibition corner. Not a side programme. A home for the conversations that actually matter.

At most conferences, the hallway is where the real business happens. Everyone knows this. Nobody designs for it.

The Summer Opportunity Lounge is a community space within ITW's communal area — built around four Living Rooms where EU-funded projects, networks, associations, and partners can meet each other and the wider ITW audience.

Every participant is tagged to an ITW Domain Track and Innovation Pillar — so the visibility you get isn't generic. It's targeted. The person walking up to your Living Room is there because the system helped them find you.

The living rooms

Four rooms. Two days. Your anchor point.

The Lounge is structured around four Living Rooms in ITW's communal space. Each one is a bookable, branded meeting area where a partner can host introductions, present initiatives, or simply be visible and available for conversation. (Day 1 is the Vision & Impact conference — the Living Rooms run on Days 2 and 3.)

What happens in the living rooms

Plugged into ITW. Not bolted on.

The Lounge runs on ITW's existing programme and matchmaking infrastructure. Nothing parallel, nothing custom — just dedicated moments within what's already happening.

🛋️ Meet the Community

Introductions that lead to collaborations.

In a Living Room slot, associations and networks introduce themselves, share open calls, and invite collaboration. The atmosphere is deliberately gezellig — a typically Dutch quality that's hard to translate, somewhere between cosy, convivial, and warm — designed so that both dealmaking and genuine socialising are equally well served.

No long pitches. Real connections.

🧭 Matching Minds

No scheduling required. Just show up and connect.

The space around the Living Rooms is designed to spark conversations. Signage divides the communal area into three zones based on what people are looking for. People group up around what they need.

The three zones

Three signals. One honest space.

Walk in, look at the signs, stand where you belong. The right conversation finds you.

1

I'm looking for a team, a consortium, or a co-founder

You have an idea, a project, or funding — and you need people. This zone is where builders find each other.

2

Tech is going too fast for the conversation

Ethics, policy, regulation, society. You're here because the questions matter as much as the answers. Find your thinking partners.

3

I fudged up. I need help. I'm stuck.

The most honest zone at any conference. You hit a wall and you need someone who's been there. No judgment — just solutions from peers.

What happens around the lounge

The Lounge is the anchor. Everything around it drives people toward connection.

The Lounge doesn't exist in isolation. It sits within ITW's wider ecosystem of activity. Adjacent to the Living Rooms:

📍

Booths

ITW's exhibition floor offers options ranging from stand-up tables for informal meetings to 2×2m, 3×3m, or custom-build setups. Book a booth to extend your physical presence with demos, materials, and face-to-face availability throughout the day.

🎤

Presentation Stage

The official ITW conference programme across 4 Domain Tracks and 6 Innovation Pillars. Lounge participants can be featured within this programme — feeding traffic and attention back into the Lounge area.

🔄

Roundtables

Intimate, focused sessions linked to ITW's Domain Tracks and Innovation Pillars. Designed for in-depth conversation and collaborative problem-solving. Lounge participants can host or join these.
