What is this track about

Not hardware. Not platforms. Meaning.

Immersive storytelling is no longer experimental. It is shaping how society feels, remembers, and questions technology. This track is where artists, designers, and cultural pioneers define what immersive media actually means — beyond hype, beyond spectacle.

Most conversations about immersive tech focus on hardware, platforms, and scale. This track focuses on meaning. On authorship. On collective presence.

How do we design agency without losing narrative power? How do artists collaborate with institutions without becoming instrumentalized? How do we work with AI without losing human intention?

Focus areas

Three pillars. One cultural shift.

Each focus area represents a critical frontier where immersive media is redefining how culture is created, experienced, and sustained.

01

Future of Storytelling & Agency

Authorship, participation, dramaturgy, and immersive language formation. How do we give audiences agency without losing the power of narrative?

02

Process, Evaluation & Collaboration Models

Funding, prototyping, institutional collaboration, and sustainable creation. The infrastructure that lets immersive art exist beyond a single project cycle.

03

Hybridity, AI & Responsible Creation

Human-AI collaboration, ethics, and hybrid physical-digital experiences. Working with AI without surrendering human intention and creative authorship.

Inspiring Tomorrow's World

Immersive art is not content. It is cultural infrastructure. It determines how emerging technologies are understood — emotionally, socially, politically. If we want to inspire tomorrow's world, creators must be at the center of that conversation.
