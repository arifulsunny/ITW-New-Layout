What is this track about

Not experimental wellness. Healthcare infrastructure.

Immersive technology in healthcare has moved from experimental wellness to primary treatment. Now we confront the real question: how do we deploy it responsibly, accessibly, and at scale?

Healthcare systems are under pressure — long waiting times, rising costs, limited access. Immersive tools are proving to be cheaper, more engaging, and evidence-based.

But technology alone is not the answer. Deployment, integration, and trust are.
